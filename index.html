YouTube Push to Play!

Enter YouTube URL:

Keyboard shortcuts:
Space:   Hold to play
Home:   Go to start
PgUp:   Go back 1 minute
PgDn:   Go forward 1 minute
↑ :   Go back 5 seconds
↓ :   Go forward 5 seconds
←:   Go back 1 seconds
→:   Play for 1 second
J:   Go back 10 seconds
K:   Play continuously
L:   Go forward 10 seconds
M:   Mute/unmute
 
Video will pause automatically
when you leave the tab.
 
With the mouse cursor in the center,
use scroll wheel to play or scrub
through the video.
